Our Story

About
Kalashree Printers

✦

"Crafting timeless invitations for your most cherished moments since 1986."

Nestled in the heart of Nagda, Madhya Pradesh, Kalashree Printers has spent nearly four decades perfecting the art of invitation design. What began as a small family workshop has grown into the region's most trusted name for luxury wedding cards and event stationery.

Every card that leaves our studio carries with it a piece of our passion — hand-picked materials, meticulous printing, and designs that honour the sanctity of your celebrations.

  • Free delivery on orders above ₹6,000
  • Minimum order: 100 cards per design
  • Open daily: 9 AM – 8 PM
  • Customisation available on all designs
